# Initial Jobless Claims
  **What exactly**? A measure of the number of individuals filing first-time claims for state unemployment insurance.
**Source**: Labor Department
**Frequency**: Weekly
**Delivered when**? Thursday at 8:30 a.m. Eastern. Data for week ended prior Saturday.
**Market importance**: Some. Sporadically moves market. Timely. Considered a decent check of the condition of the labor market and great indicator of the tone of the employment report.
**Other notes**: Series is unstable and subject to big corrections. The four-week average is utilized to check the underlying trend in claims.
