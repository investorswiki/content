# Economic Indicator
A variable, for example, the unemployment rate or volume of help-wanted advertising, that shows the course of the economy.

## Features
- Indicators can be driving — which will more often than not go before trends, slacking — which affirm trends, or incidental — that which is occurring now.
- The most generally utilized economic indicators come from data delivered by the government and non-benefit organizations or universities.
- An economic indicator is a macroeconomic measurement utilized by analysts to grasp current and future economic activity and opportunity.
