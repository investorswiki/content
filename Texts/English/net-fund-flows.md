# Net Fund Flows
Followed by several unique companies, fund flows address the amount of new money coming into mutual funds less the amount of money "recovered" by investors. However an emphatically vague science, fund flow tracking has turned into a cottage industry, as market watchers utilize the figures to measure public sentiment toward the market and liquidity.
