# Voluntary Trust
## What Is a Voluntary Trust?

A voluntary trust is a type of [living trust](/living-trust) that is made during the lifetime of the trustor and is otherwise called a [inter vivos trust](/intervivostrust). In a voluntary trust, the trustor holds the legal title of the gift moved to the [beneficiary](/beneficiary), even however the beneficiary has genuine title and possession and ability to carry out activities of the trust. A voluntary trust is likewise defined as an obligation emerging out of a personal confidence rested in, and deliberately accepted by, one individual for the benefit of another. This is as opposed to an involuntary trust, which is made by law.

## Figuring out Voluntary Trusts

Voluntary trusts are frequently utilized in [estate planning](/estateplanning). In the circumstances of real estate, the person who controls the real estate property is known as the trustee, while the individual who will receive the property after it is being held is known as the [trustor](/trustor). The property itself is alluded to as the res.

In a voluntary trust, no consideration is made. In a voluntary trust, the beneficiary of the trust doesn't give anything in exchange for the trust however receives it as a pure gift. By abstaining from getting something in return, voluntary trusts are recognized from trusts for value, which are trusts made for buyers and mortgagees. Something contrary to a voluntary trust is an involuntary trust, which can be additionally broken down into constructive or coming about trusts.

At times, a voluntary trust can likewise basically allude to the interpersonal confidence between two individuals in regards to an obligation for the benefit of one another, or one individual in the relationship. Nonetheless, in legal terms, a voluntary trust is a formal structural setup that can manage an organization's activities and, at times, its financial operations.

## Illustration of a Voluntary Trust

Many types of charitable and service organizations operate voluntary funds to keep up with operations. To act as an illustration of how a voluntary trust can function, one can take a gander at the Trust Fund of the United Nations Alliance of Civilizations. Through a Special Report made by the organization's High-Level Group, it was suggested the Alliance lay out a voluntary trust fund. As the organization makes sense of by means of its website, the primary objectives of the Trust Fund are to support the ventures and activities of the Alliance of Civilizations, the activities and effort drives attempted by the High Representative in his official capacity and help with core operational and human resources needs.
