# Bill Gross

Bill Gross is an eminent bond investor who helped to establish the Pacific Investment Management Company, PIMCO, and is known as the "Bond King."

Gross is the creator of **Bill Gross on Investing, Everything You've Heard About Investing Is Wrong!** **I'm Still Standing**.

## Early Life and Education

Bill Gross was brought into the world on April 13, 1944, in Middletown, Ohio. He earned a four year certification in psychology from Duke University in 1966 and served in the United States Navy during the Vietnam War.

A risk-taker, Bill Gross spent his initial career gambling and counting cards at poker tables before joining Pacific Mutual Life in Los Angeles as an investment analyst. In 1971, he established his firm, Pacific Investment Management Company, [PIMCO](/pimco), in Newport Beach, California, with friends Jim Muzzy and Bill Podlich.

Bill Gross is credited with aggressive bond investing and transforming the quiet bond market into an undermined game of high risk and reward. Overseeing assets of almost $2 trillion out of 2022, PIMCO is the biggest active fixed income fund management firm in the world.

In 2014, Bill Gross left PIMCO during a period of internal management battles. He in the long run sued and settled with PIMCO following his expelling.
