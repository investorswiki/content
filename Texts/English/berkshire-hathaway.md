Buffett assumed control of the striving New England company in 1965. Since that time, Berkshire has become quite possibly of the largest company in the world, in light of market capitalization.

### What Is a Class A Share?
Class A shares of common stock ordinarily give shareholders a greater amount of voting rights than Class B and different classes of stock. They're much of the time held by a company's executives and a few individuals from management so those in charge of the company can hold control of it in different circumstances, for example, a hostile takeover endeavor.

### What Is Value Investing?
Value investing alludes to investing in a security with a [intrinsic value](/intrinsicvalue) that is greater than its market value. The thought is that the undervalued security's market value ought to increase to meet its intrinsic value. Warren Buffett is one illustration of an investor whose emphasis on value investing has prompted mind boggling achievement.
