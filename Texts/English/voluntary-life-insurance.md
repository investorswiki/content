# Voluntary Life Insurance
## What Is Voluntary Life Insurance?

Voluntary life insurance is a financial protection plan that gives a cash benefit to a beneficiary upon the death of the insured. It's an optional benefit offered by employers. The employee pays a month to month premium in exchange for the back up plan's guarantee of payment upon the insured's death.

Employer sponsorship generally makes premiums for voluntary life insurance policies more affordable than individual life insurance policies sold in the retail market.

## Grasping Voluntary Life Insurance

Numerous insurers furnish voluntary life insurance plans with extra benefits and [riders](/rider). For example, a plan could feature the option to purchase insurance over the guaranteed issue amount. Contingent upon the amount of increase, policyholders might be required to submit proof that they satisfy least wellbeing guidelines.

Another is coverage [portability,](/portability) which is the ability of a policyholder to proceed with the life endless supply of employment. Every employer has rules for porting a policy. Nonetheless, it is normally somewhere in the range of 30 and 60 days after termination, and it requires the completion of desk work.

A third option is the ability to [accelerate benefits](/accelerated-benefits), by which the death benefit is paid during the life of the insured in the event that they are declared terminally ill. There is likewise the option to purchase life insurance for spouses, domestic partners, and dependents, as defined by the insurance company.

Finally, an inconceivable benefit offered by most employers is the option to deduct premiums from salary. Payroll deductions are helpful for the employee and consider the easy and convenient payment of premiums.

## Types of Voluntary Life Insurance

There are two types of voluntary life insurance policies given by employers: voluntary whole life and voluntary term life. The last option is otherwise called [group term life insurance](/group-term-life-insurance). Face amounts might be in multiples of an employee's salary or stated values, for example, $20,000, $50,000, or $100,000.

### Voluntary Whole Life Insurance

Voluntary whole life safeguards the whole life of the insured. On the off chance that whole life coverage is chosen for a spouse or dependent, the policy safeguards that individual's whole life too. Regularly, amounts for spouses and dependents are not as much as amounts accessible for employees.

Just similarly as with permanent whole life policies, cash value aggregates as per the underlying investments. A few policies just apply a fixed rate of interest to the cash value, while others consider variable investing in [equity funds](/equityfund).

### Voluntary Term Life Insurance

Voluntary term life insurance is a policy that offers protection for a limited period, like five, 10, or 20 years. Building cash value and variable investing are not qualities of voluntary term insurance. Subsequently, premiums are more affordable than their whole life equivalents. Premiums are level during the policy term however can increase upon renewal.

> Voluntary life is frequently paid with pre-tax dollars. In the event that it is paid with after-tax dollars it could be tax-deductible.
>

## FAQ
### What Is the Difference Between Group Term and Voluntary Term Life Insurance?
Voluntary life insurance and group life insurance are frequently utilized conversely.
### Is Voluntary Term Life Group Insurance?
Indeed. Voluntary life insurance is covered by means of a group policy put in place by an organization. Along these lines, most individual employees can purchase a policy under the umbrella plan without underwriting or a medical exam. Furthermore, the cost of the premiums will commonly be not exactly for an individual policy.
### The amount Voluntary Term Life Insurance Do I Need?
While you might need or need a bigger death benefit, voluntary term life is generally limited by an employer to either 1x-2x the amount of your annual compensation. Different companies will set a cap at between $50,000 - $250,000 in coverage.
### What Is Voluntary Dependent Life Insurance?
This employee benefit can cover a spouse, children, and some other eligible dependents, contingent on the rules spread out in the plan. If a dependent kicks the bucket, the employee would receive the death benefit.
