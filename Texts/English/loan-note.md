# Loan Note
## What Is a Loan Note?

A loan note is an extended form of a generic [I Owe You](/iou) (IOU) document starting with one party then onto the next. It enables a [payee](/payee) (borrower) to receive payments from a lender, conceivably with an interest rate connected, over a set period of time, and ending on the date at which the whole loan is to be repaid. Loan notes are normally given in lieu of cash at the payee's request.

A loan note denotes a type of contract that normally outlines the legal obligations of the lender and the borrower. A legitimate loan note will incorporate a set of contractual punishments, including the right to sue or look for [arbitration](/arbitration) if either party to the contract neglects to meet or in any case [defaults](/default2) on financial obligations.

## How a Loan Note Works

A loan note, a form of [promissory agreement](/promissorynote), incorporates all of the associated loan terms. It is viewed as a legally binding agreement with the two players considered committed to the terms as they are written. A loan note can be drawn up by one or the other borrower or lender, however it is all the more generally completed by the lender. The note is viewed as substantial until the amount listed on the document is paid in full by the borrower.

## Information Contained in a Loan Note

The loan note contains each of the relevant insights about the legal agreement arrived at by the elaborate gatherings. This incorporates the names and contact information for the two players, as well as the [principal](/principal) balance and any [interest rate](/interestrate) being applied over the term of the loan. Extra information with respect to the payment schedule, including the due date, will be incorporated.

Penalty information may likewise be incorporated. This can incorporate repercussions for late payments or information relating to [prepayment penalties](/prepaymentpenalty).

> While prepayment clauses act as a protection to the lender against losing interest income throughout the span of the loan, the clause means the borrower will pay a penalty for paying down or paying off the loan during a predetermined time, for the most part inside the initial not many long periods of the loan's beginning date.
>
## Illustration of a Loan Note

Loan notes are utilized generally for [installment debts](/installmentdebt) in which the borrower is making a purchase of a specific decent or service and paying the amount back over the long run. A common consumer need for a loan note is a home purchase, which incorporates a loan note alongside the mortgage or [deed of trust](/trustdeed). Loan notes are likewise utilized for vehicle financing and most different forms of installment loans.

## Benefits of Loan Notes

A loan note can assist an individual with avoiding an undue tax hit due to a lump-sum payment from a settlement or cash-out package from a company. In these cases, the individual is given a decision between cash or a loan note. At the point when loan notes are utilized between businesses, the purchaser can act as a borrower and make payments after some time, frequently at a negligible interest rate.

Loan notes can be genuinely simple to draw up and advantageous for the two players to carry out with direct subtleties. They can address the primary form of funding for youthful businesses. Startups and new entrepreneurs frequently use them to get [seed capital](/seedcapital) from friends and family to lay out a business.

## Special Considerations for Loan Notes

Legally, a loan note holds more significance than an informal IOU, even when the informal IOU is [notarized](/legally approve). Generally, a loan note will be maintained except if either party can demonstrate the agreement was placed into while [under duress](/duress), which might make the conditions inside the document void, delivering them unenforceable.
