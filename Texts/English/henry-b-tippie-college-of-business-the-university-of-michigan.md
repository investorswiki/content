# University of Iowa Tippie College of Business
## What Is the University of Iowa Tippie College of Business?

The University of Iowa Tippie College of Business is the business school at the University of Iowa in Iowa City, Iowa. Established in 1921, it offers both undergraduate and graduate programs.

Prior to 2019, the University of Iowa Tippie College of Business was notable for its [Master of Business Administration (MBA)](/mba), which had been rated as the best finance-centered MBA program in the world by the Financial Times in 2016. In 2019, the school graduated its last companion of full-time MBA students, in spite of the fact that it keeps on offering a Professional MBA program for those wishing to seek after their MBA on a parttime basis.

## Figuring out the University of Iowa Tippie College of Business

Recently known as the University of Iowa College of Commerce, the school became known as the University of Iowa Tippie College of Business in 1999, following a naming gift by the [entrepreneur](/entrepreneur) and humanitarian, Henry B. Tippie.

Today, the University of Iowa Tippie College of Business is home to more than 4,000 students, remembering 3,000 undergraduate students and 1,500 students for graduate-level programs. The college's 450 employees keep up with dedicated research centers in subjects, for example, [accounting](/financialaccounting), [economics](/economics), and [finance](/finance). The school is likewise home to the renowned Iowa Electronic Markets (IEM), which is a leading research institution for the study of prediction markets.

As well as offering undergraduate majors in the core business disciplines of accounting, economics, finance, and [marketing](/marketing), the University of Iowa Tippie College of Business likewise offers a professional MBA program intended for students wishing to complete their MBAs while continuing to advance their careers. Albeit this program is offered at four areas all through the state of Iowa, extra programs are accessible for those wishing to study from a distance or abroad. This incorporates an online MBA program, as well as international MBA programs situated in Italy and Hong Kong.

Graduate students who would rather not seek after a MBA can decide to do a Master of Accountancy (MAc) degree, a Master's and Certificate in Business Analytics, or a Master of Finance.
