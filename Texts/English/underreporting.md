# Under Reporting
## What Is Under Reporting?

Under reporting is a term depicting the crime of intentionally reporting less [income](/income) or revenue than was actually received. Companies and individuals essentially under report their approaches with an end goal to keep away from or reduce their separate [tax liabilities](/taxliability).

Under reporting is definitely not a harmless crime. In fact, the billions of dollars of tax-loss revenue brought about by under reporting reduces the funds the federal government depends on to finance [Social Security](/socialsecurity), [Medicare](/medicare), and a large group of different programs.
