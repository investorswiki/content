# Marginal Rate of Technical Substitution
## What Is the Marginal Rate of Technical Substitution - MRTS?

The marginal rate of technical substitution (MRTS) is an economic theory that illustrates the rate at which one factor must diminish so a similar level of productivity can be kept up with when another factor is increased.

The MRTS mirrors the compromise between factors, for example, capital and labor, that permit a firm to keep a consistent output. MRTS varies from the [marginal rate of substitution (MRS)](/marginal_rate_substitution) on the grounds that MRTS is centered around producer [equilibrium](/equilibrium) and MRS is centered around consumer equilibrium.
