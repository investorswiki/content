# UNC Kenan-Flagler Business School
## What Is UNC Kenan-Flagler Business School?

UNC Kenan-Flagler Business School is the [business school](/b-school) of the University of North Carolina. Founded in 1919 and situated in Chapel Hill, North Carolina, the school offers both undergraduate and graduate programs.

The UNC Kenan-Flagler Business School is notable for its [Master of Business Administration (MBA)](/mba) program, which places a high level of accentuation on leadership ability. It is commonly positioned among the 30 best MBA programs worldwide by leading distributions.
