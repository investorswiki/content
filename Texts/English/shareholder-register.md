# Shareholder Register
## What Is a Shareholder Register?

A shareholder register is a rundown of active owners of a company's shares, refreshed on a continuous basis. The [shareholder](/shareholder) register expects that each current shareholder is recorded. The register incorporates every individual's name, address, and the number of shares owned. Likewise, the register can detail the holder's occupation and the price they paid for the shares.

The shareholder register is fundamental to the examination of the ownership of a company. Shareholder register is a term utilized in Europe and different parts of the world, while the term utilized in the U.S. is shareholder list.

## How a Shareholder Register Works

A shareholder register must note all shares issued by a company. Moreover, it ought to detail any potential limitations on transferring shares, along with significant references, if accessible. For each [share class](/share_class), the register must likewise list shareholders by name, in sequential order, and each party's last known physical address.

Some shareholder registers venture to detail all issues of shares to every individual shareholder in the last 10 years, along with the date of all possible transfers of shares. This can likewise incorporate the name of the party to whom shares have been moved.

The shareholder register ought to incorporate the purchase prices of these shares, too. In the event that shares are not completely paid for, the register must note the unpaid amount.

## Special Considerations

Extra critical parts of company record keeping incorporate a current and projected [capital structure](/capitalstructure). This document, frequently in an Excel file, subtleties the financing of a company's current operations and future objectives for growth.

Wellsprings of funds can emerge out of giving [equity](/equity) (new shares of which would be noted in real-time in the shareholder register), and [debt](/debt). Equity can be as common or [preferred stock](/preferredstock), while debt can be short-term or long-term in nature.
