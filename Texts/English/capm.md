# Capital Asset Pricing Model (CAPM)
Capital asset pricing model (CAPM) gives a snapshot to investors of the relationship among risk and anticipated return, particularly applied to risk-based securities. The model is intended to help investors with navigation and investment determination based on whether the stock will meet or surpass the expected rate of return.
