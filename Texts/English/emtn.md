# Euro Medium Term Note - EMTN
## What Is an Euro Medium Term Note?

An euro medium-term note is a medium-term, flexible [debt instrument](/debtinstrument) that is traded and issued outside of the United States and Canada. These instruments require fixed payments and are straightforwardly issued to the market with [maturities](/maturity) that are under five years. EMTNs permit an issuer to enter the foreign markets all the more effectively to get capital. Firms likewise offer EMTNs ceaselessly, while a bond issue, for instance, happens at the same time.
