# IRS Form 4868
## What Is Form 4868: Application for Automatic Extension of Time to File U.S. Individual Income Tax Return?

Form 4868: Application for Automatic Extension of Time to File U.S. Individual Income Tax Return is an Internal Revenue Service (IRS) form for individuals who wish to expand the amount of time they need to file their tax returns.

Any extension granted by the IRS applies just to the administrative work. You must send in the amount you hope to owe by the cutoff time.

> Filing Form 4868 doesn't permit late payment. All payments must be made by the tax cutoff time date or interest and punishments will be forced.
>
## Who Can File Form 4868: Application for Automatic Extension of Time to File U.S. Individual Income Tax Return?

A U.S. citizen or resident who needs an automatic [extension](/filing-extension) to file a U.S. individual income [tax return](/taxreturn) can file this form. Taxpayers in the country receive a maximum half year extension. U.S. citizens or residents who are out of the country on the due date are permitted two extra months without Form 4868 to file their returns and pay any amount due without requesting an extension. Filing this form will grant four extra months to the taxpayer living out of the country. The IRS qualifies those "out of the country" as:

- Anybody who lives outside the U.S. furthermore, Puerto Rico and your fundamental place of work is outside the U.S. furthermore, Puerto Rico
- Anybody in military or maritime service on duty outside the U.S. furthermore, Puerto Rico

## Step by step instructions to File Form 4868: Application for Automatic Extension of Time to File U.S. Individual Income Tax Return

This short form ought to be completed with the individual or two or three's names, address, Social Security numbers, an estimate of total tax liability for the year, total payments previously made, the leftover amount due, and the amount being paid, if any.

The form ought to be filed by the tax cutoff time date.
