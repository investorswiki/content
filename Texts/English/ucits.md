# Undertakings Collective Investment in Transferable Securities (UCITS)
## What Is UCITS?

The Undertakings for the Collective Investment in Transferable Securities (UCITS) is a regulatory structure of the European Commission that makes a harmonized system all through Europe for the management and sale of mutual funds. UCITS funds can be registered in Europe and sold to investors worldwide utilizing unified regulatory and investor protection requirements. UCITS fund suppliers who fulfill the guidelines are exempt from national regulation in individual European countries.

## Figuring out UCITS

In ordinary utilization, a UCITS is a mutual fund situated in the European Union. UCITS funds are perceived as safe and very much regulated investments and are famous in Europe, South America, and Asia among investors who don't really want to invest in a single [public limited company](/plc) yet rather among diversified unit trusts spread out inside the European Union.

## History of UCITS

The principal UCITS Directive was adopted on Dec. 20, 1985, with a stated aim of facilitating cross-border offerings of investment funds to retail investors. In the mid 1990s, recommendations for changes to the directive were made however never completely adopted. Thusly, there is no UCITS II. Be that as it may, in 2002, following conversations among member countries, two new directives were adopted. Directives 2001/107/EC and 2001/108/EC, together known as UCITS III, widened the investment range of UCITS funds and loosened up certain limitations for index funds.

UCITS IV, or Directive 2009/65/EC, brought about additional technical changes and was adopted in July 2011. At long last, UCITS V, or Directive 2014/91/EU, which came full circle in March 2016, adjusts fund safes' duties and obligations and fund managers' remuneration requirements with those of the [Alternative Investment Fund Managers Directive](/alternative-investment-fund-managers-directive-aifmd) (AIFMD).

Since they are viewed as exceptionally safe and all around regulated, UCITS funds are extremely famous investments. As per the European Commission, they account for around 75% of all collective investments by small investors in Europe. Numerous mutual fund suppliers utilize an articulation, for example, "UCITS-agreeable" as part of their marketing strategy. While the funds are regulated in Europe, purchasers from everywhere the world can invest in UCITS funds.
